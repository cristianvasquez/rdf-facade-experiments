🔄 Markdown to RDF Playground

Literate RDF authoring through markdown: author semantic graphs using familiar markdown syntax. The facade is a general intermediate representation of the markdown structure. The SPARQL CONSTRUCT mapping makes the semantics explicit, transforming the facade into domain RDF. Same semantic meaning, different syntactic styles—you choose what fits your workflow.
